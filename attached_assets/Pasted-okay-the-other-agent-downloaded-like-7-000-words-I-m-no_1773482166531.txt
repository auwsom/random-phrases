## POS Labeling Options
- **Keep simple**: Split your list by length/common patterns (short nouns vs. action verbs), but for accuracy:
  1. Use free POS taggers: Feed words into `nltk.pos_tag()` (Python) or spaCy—outputs categories like NN (noun), JJ (adj), VB (verb).
  2. Download ready lists: Google "Brown Corpus POS-tagged words" or "Universal Dependencies English sample" (thousands free, categorized instantly).
- Your agent can run: `import nltk; nltk.download('averaged_perceptron_tagger'); tags = nltk.pos_tag(your_words_list)` → auto-categorize into dicts like `nouns`, `verbs`.
